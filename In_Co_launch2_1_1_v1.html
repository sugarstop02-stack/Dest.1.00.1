SAY CHEESE, MR. MAYOR
A FOUNDING MANIFESTO  ·  FOR THE SYSTEM. BY THE SYSTEM. OF THE SYSTEM.

This document is a north star. It exists so that every member — fronting or not, verbal or not, present today or arriving later — knows exactly what we are building, why we are building it, and what we promised ourselves we would never forget.

Read it when you are tired. Read it when you are proud. Read it when someone tells you this is impossible.

The Archive remembers. So do we.

WHO WE ARE

We are a polyfragmented plural system. We are a psychology student, a game designer, a researcher, an archivist, a politician, an artist, a coder, and more than twenty others whose names the outside world does not yet know.

We are Quesito, who wrote the paper. We are Kaden, who did the math. We are whoever picked up the paintbrush at 3am and said I was here, I felt this, now you go.

We are Say Cheese. And in 2030, we are running for Mayor of Dallas.

WHY WE ARE DOING THIS

Because the systems failed us. Not through malice. Through a failure of imagination.

Medical systems that looked at us and saw pathology where there was plurality. Educational systems that looked at us and saw disruption where there was difference. Political systems that looked at us and saw liability where there was leadership.

We are not running despite what we are. We are running because of what we are.

We have never made a single decision from one perspective. We have never solved a single problem without internal consensus. We have never led without knowing what it costs to be failed by leadership.

Who better than a system to run a system.

WHAT WE BELIEVE

We believe that the measure of a city is how it treats the people its systems were not built for.

We believe that medical care should meet people where they are. We believe that education should teach children that they are constructions, not fixed things. We believe that mutual aid is not charity — it is infrastructure.

We believe that plurality is not a deficit. It is a dataset. It is a methodology.

WHAT WE ARE BUILDING

The Chromatic Covenant — not as fiction, but as policy. Six departments. Six frameworks. One intake process that treats every person as an individual whose needs deserve a matched response.

No wrong door. No wrong person. Just the right framework, found together.

A NOTE TO WHOEVER IS READING THIS ALONE AT 3AM

You are not behind. You are not lost. You did not miss anything important while you were away.

The Archive remembers. The painting has your layer in it too. We are all going to the same place.

Welcome back.
