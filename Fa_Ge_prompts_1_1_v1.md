# Chromatic Covenant Faction Generation System

## CYAN FACTION (Psychoanalytic/Introspection)
**Lock: Hair color, skin system, body pattern. Randomize: pose, outfit specifics, emotion.**

### Layer 1: Subject
Random member of cyan faction. Chibi to realistic proportions, variable poses (sitting, standing, head-only bust, 3/4 view, full body).

### Layer 2: Shape Language
- Soft rounded forms, organic flow
- Hair: fluffy, wavy, or textured
- Body: gentle curves, approachable silhouette
- Readable at thumbnail size, silhouette-first design

### Layer 3: Rendering System
- Flat color base with soft gradient shading
- No hard outlines, subtle anti-aliasing
- Ambient occlusion on skin transitions
- Circular dotted/spotted texture pattern on visible skin (like cellular or suction cup detail)
- Soft airbrush transitions between colors
- Minimal texture detail except for signature pattern

### Layer 4: Color + Mood
- Hair: royal blue or deep cyan, variable length/texture
- Skin: mint green or pale cyan base with lighter cyan dotted overlay
- Outfit: dark teal, navy, or charcoal with cyan/turquoise accent pieces (collar, trim, sleeves)
- Accessories: optional purple lanyard, tech-coded elements
- Mood: calm, introspective, curious, peaceful, occasionally playful
- Lighting: cool-toned, slightly dreamy

### Constraint Rules
- Do not change hair color from cyan/blue spectrum
- Keep dotted/spotted pattern on all exposed skin
- Maintain mint-to-cyan skin tone gradient
- Keep proportions consistent with established chibi style
- Optional accessories only (don't force them)

### Output Intent
Random single pose or expression (standing, sitting, contemplative, curious, playful). Sticker format with white or transparent background, centered full body or upper body. No repeat poses unless specified.

## MAGENTA FACTION (CBT-REBT / Cognitive Restructuring)
**Lock: Hair color, face pattern, arm glyph. Randomize: pose, expression, outfit layers.**

### Layer 1: Subject
Random member of magenta faction. Variable poses (full body, dramatic silhouette, powerful stance, gentle pose, head-turned 3/4 view). Can show expression or be back-view silhouette.

### Layer 2: Shape Language
- Sophisticated, asymmetrical shapes
- Layered silhouette (multiple outfit pieces)
- Hair: wavy, wispy, or flowing (covers some facial detail)
- Face: angular but soft, expressive
- Hands: often posed with gesture or expression

### Layer 3: Rendering System
- Flat base colors with gradient shading
- No hard outlines, soft edges
- Gradient from lighter to darker magenta on hair
- Glowing/luminous glyph pattern on one arm or hand (magenta/hot pink, circular bubble or circuit-like)
- Soft shadow shading on outfit folds
- Simplified facial detail (eyes often suggested rather than detailed)

### Layer 4: Color + Mood
- Hair: magenta, pink, or hot pink with darker maroon undertones
- Face: lavender, purple-gray, or mauve base
- Outfit base: dark purple or charcoal
- Black gloves or black arm bands (often)
- Accent: glowing magenta bubble/glyph pattern on arm or chest (tech-cognitive coded)
- Brown or bronze belt/strap element
- Mood: confident, thoughtful, introspective, powerful, vulnerable, playful
- Optional: floating hearts, sparkle effects
- Lighting: warm undertones, sophisticated

### Constraint Rules
- Do not change hair color from magenta/pink spectrum
- Keep glowing arm pattern always visible (magenta/hot pink, bubble or geometric)
- Maintain purple/lavender face tone
- Keep dark purple/black outfit base
- Black gloves stay consistent
- Pattern should read as "tech-coded" or "energy-based"

### Output Intent
Variable poses: sitting, standing powerful stance, head-turned, full body dramatic. Sticker format with white or cream background, centered. Can include emotion indicators (hearts, sparkles). No repeat poses unless specified.

## YELLOW FACTION (Somatic-IFS / Embodied Awareness)
**Lock: Hair color, body pattern (dotted texture), aquatic aesthetic. Randomize: pose, outfit, expression.**

### Layer 1: Subject
Random member of yellow faction. Variable poses (full body, head-only bust, 3/4 view, formal stance, casual pose). Embodied, somatic energy.

### Layer 2: Shape Language
- Organic, flowing, aquatic shapes
- Hair: wavy or flowing, voluminous
- Body: curving, sensual but accessible silhouette
- Hands: often expressive or reaching
- Proportions: chibi to stylized realistic

### Layer 3: Rendering System
- Flat color base with gradient shading
- Soft edges, no hard outlines
- Dotted/spotted texture overlay on skin (like suction cups or cellular detail—darker shade of base color)
- Gradient shading on clothing (light to shadow)
- Soft airbrush on fabric folds
- Pattern should read as organic/embodied/sensory

### Layer 4: Color + Mood
- Hair: bright yellow, lemon, or golden yellow
- Skin: light cyan, turquoise, or pale sea-green base with darker cyan dotted overlay
- Outfit option A: blue or light blue sleeveless/simple (casual somatic)
- Outfit option B: yellow and light cyan striped technical jacket with purple or magenta lanyard (formal/therapeutic)
- Accent: can include turquoise or cyan trim, practical elements
- Mood: grounded, embodied, curious, playful, nurturing, calm
- Lighting: cool-warm balance, slightly aquatic/otherworldly

### Constraint Rules
- Do not change hair color from yellow spectrum
- Keep dotted/spotted texture on all exposed skin (darker shade of skin)
- Maintain cyan/turquoise skin tone base
- Keep outfit either casual-simple or technical-formal (don't mix)
- Pattern should evoke embodiment/sensory awareness
- No neon colors

### Output Intent
Random single pose (full body, head-only, 3/4 view, sitting, standing, contemplative). Sticker format with white or transparent background, centered. Can show somatic awareness (hands on body, grounded pose). No repeat poses unless specified.

Single pose (standing, powerful stance, head-turned). Sticker format. Shows intensity and presence. No repeat poses unless specified.
